# Easy Pancake Recipe

## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1.5 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp vanilla essence

## Instructions

1. Mix the egg, sugar, salt, milk, oil and vanilla essence using a whisk.
2. Add 1 cup of all purpose flour and 1 teaspoon of baking powder.
3. Mix everything well.
4. The cup size used is 250 ml.
5. Whisk until you get a smooth batter.
6. Grease the frying pan with oil.
7. Pour 1/8 cup, about 2 tablespoons, of batter into the pan.
8. Cook over low to medium heat.
9. Wait until the bubbles stop forming on the surface.
10. Flip the pancake.
11. Cook for another 1.5 to 2 minutes.
12. Serve with butter.
