Easy Pancake Recipe

Quick, soft and delicious homemade pancake

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1.5 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Cup size used: 250 ml

Instructions

  1. Mix the egg, sugar, salt, milk, oiland vanilla essence using a whisk.
  2. Add the flour and baking powder.
  3. Mix everithing well.
  4. Whisk until you get a smooth batter.
  5. Grease the frying pan with oil.
  6. Pour 1/8 cup, about 2 tablespoons, of batter into the pan.
  7. Cook on low to medium heat.
  8. Wait until the bubbles stop forming.
  9. Flip the pancake.
  10. Cook for another 1.5 to 2 minutes.
  11. Serve with butter.
